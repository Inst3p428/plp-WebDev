Nissan Gt-r R32

The Nissan GT-R R32, also known as the "Godzilla", is one of the most legendary Japanese sports cars ever made. It was produced between 1989 and 1994 and played a significant role in establishing Nissan’s dominance in motorsports.

Key Features & Specs

  1. Engine – 2.6L RB26DETT Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6
  2. Power Output – 276 HP
  3. Torque – 353 Nm
  4. Transmission – 5-speed manual
  5. Drivetrain – ATTESA E-TS AWD system
  6. Weight – Around 1,430 kg
  7. 0-100 km/h – ~5 seconds

Fun Facts

  • The R32 GT-R was originally limited to Japan but gained global fame through imports.
  • It became an icon in pop culture, featuring in games like Gran Turismo, Forza, and Need for Speed.
  • The RB26 engine is legendary for tuning, with many builds easily exceeding 1,000 HP.
