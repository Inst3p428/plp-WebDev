Porsche 911 Turbo (930)

The Porsche 911 Turbo (930) is a legendary sports car produced from 1975 to 1989. Known for its distinctive wide rear fenders, "whale tail" spoiler, and thrilling turbocharged performance, the 930 became an icon of its era.

Powered by a 3.0L and later a 3.3L flat-six engine with a turbocharger, the 930 delivered groundbreaking performance and introduced the world to turbo lag. It combined luxury and aggression in one unique package, setting a standard for high-performance vehicles.
